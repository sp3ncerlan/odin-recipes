Prime Rib

Description

This is a delectable, juicy prime rib.

First by slow roasting, then blasting it with the broiler.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 pound) prime rib loin roast
  • 2 cups concentrated beef base (paste)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup whole black peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Season the roast
  2. Stick it into a preheated oven for 2 hours
  3. Turn on the broiler for 15 minutes
  4. Take out and rest for 15 minutes
