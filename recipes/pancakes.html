Pancakes

Description

This is a sweet pancake.

Made by mixing together ingredients and then pan frying.

Ingredients

  • Pre-made pancake batter
  • Skillet
  • Syrup
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix batter together
  2. Heat up the skillet, add some oil
  3. Pan fry the pancake
  4. Stack cooked pancakes, the drizzle syrup
  5. Add butter pads if desired
